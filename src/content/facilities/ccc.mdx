
# NITK CCC Facilities

## Network Infrastructure
- **24x7** Campus-wide LAN and Internet connectivity
- **20 km** of 12-core OFC with **1 Gbps & 10 Gbps** backbone
- Departments, residences, hostels, and administrative offices connected to core switch

## Network Expansion Stages
- **1999**: First stage of campus network
- **2006**: Second stage (**TEQIP funds**)
- **2016**: Third stage - **Core Network Expansion & Campus WiFi** (Rs. 6.78 crores)

## Wi-Fi Network
### **Phase 1**
- **744** Ruckus R500 Indoor Access Points
- **40** Ruckus T300 Outdoor Access Points
- **5** Ruckus H500 Wall Switches
- **89** Netgear PoE Switches

### **Phase 2 (CSE Building & LHC-C)**
- **97** Ruckus R510 Indoor Access Points
- **4** Ruckus T300 Outdoor Access Points
- **11** PoE Switches

## Internet Bandwidth
- **Existing:** **5 Gbps** (1 Gbps NKN + 4 Gbps BSNL)
- **Upgrade:** **10 Gbps** with WAN-side improvements

## Data Centre & Servers
- **Located in CCC Ground Floor**
- **Virtualization:** Blade Servers (**VMWare**), Dell/Lenovo (**Proxmox & Ubuntu containerization**)
- **Eight new servers added**
