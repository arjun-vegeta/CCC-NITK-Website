# NITK Net Captive Portal Configuration  

Follow the steps below to connect to NITK Wi-Fi and LAN:  

## **Wi-Fi Setup**  
1. Connect to **NITK-NET** WiFi.  
2. Use passkey: `2K16NITK`.  
3. Open <a href="https://nac.nitk.ac.in:8090/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>NITK Captive Portal</strong></a> and log in.


## **LAN Setup**  
1. Connect LAN cable.  
2. Follow the same login process as Wi-Fi.  

> **Note:** Change your captive portal password through IRIS by clicking on your display picture and selecting the **"Change Password"** option. Ensure you update it only via the official IRIS website for a successful password change.
