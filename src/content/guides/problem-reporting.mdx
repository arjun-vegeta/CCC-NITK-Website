# Problem Reporting

## How to create a complaint

1. Go to [IRIS](https://iris.nitk.ac.in/)
2. Navigate to **Hostel Dashboard** under the **Campus** section
3. Select **Hostel Complaint** from the sidebar
4. Click on **New Complaint** to file your complaint

Alternatively, you can go directly to the complaint page:
- [Hostel Complaint Page](https://iris.nitk.ac.in/hrms/hostels/hostel_complaints)

## Read Before Reporting

Please make sure to check the troubleshooting guide before filing a complaint: [Troubleshooting Guide](/guides/guide_for_troubleshooting)

## Application Flow

1. Fill in the required fields on the complaint form.
2. Submit the complaint.
3. You can check the status of your complaint later on the **Hostel Complaint Dashboard** in IRIS.
